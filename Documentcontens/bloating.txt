Bloating and Digestive Distention in Traditional Chinese Medicine

Bloating is commonly associated with dysfunction of the spleen and stomach.

Symptoms

abdominal fullness
gas
poor appetite
belching
discomfort after meals

Common Patterns

Food Stagnation
Occurs when food is not properly digested.

Spleen Qi Deficiency
Weak digestion leading to gas and bloating.

Liver Qi Stagnation
Stress disrupting digestion.

Dampness Accumulation
Heaviness, sticky stool, and digestive sluggishness.

Treatment aims to improve digestion, move Qi, and reduce dampness.
