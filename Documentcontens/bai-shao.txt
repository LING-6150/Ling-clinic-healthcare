Bai Shao (White Peony Root)

Category
Blood nourishing herb.

Nature and Flavor
Slightly cold and bitter.

Meridians Entered
Liver and Spleen.

Functions
Nourishes blood.
Softens liver.
Relieves pain.
Regulates menstruation.

Indications
Menstrual cramps.
Muscle spasms.
Dizziness.
Irritability.

Common Formulas
Si Wu Tang
Xiao Yao San.
