Ba Zhen Tang (Eight Treasure Decoction)

Ba Zhen Tang is a classical herbal formula in Traditional Chinese Medicine used to tonify both Qi and Blood.

The name "Ba Zhen Tang" means "Eight Treasures Decoction", referring to the eight herbs included in the formula.

Ingredients

Ren Shen (Ginseng)
Bai Zhu (Atractylodes)
Fu Ling (Poria)
Gan Cao (Licorice)
Dang Gui (Angelica Sinensis)
Bai Shao (White Peony Root)
Chuan Xiong (Ligusticum Chuanxiong)
Shu Di Huang (Prepared Rehmannia Root)

Functions

Tonifies Qi
Nourishes Blood
Improves overall vitality
Supports recovery from chronic illness

Indications

fatigue
pale complexion
dizziness
shortness of breath
weakness
irregular menstruation

Comparison with Si Wu Tang

Si Wu Tang focuses primarily on nourishing blood, while Ba Zhen Tang tonifies both Qi and Blood.

Clinical Application

Ba Zhen Tang is commonly used for patients with combined Qi and Blood deficiency, especially those recovering from long-term illness or experiencing chronic fatigue.

It is also frequently used in women's health for conditions related to blood deficiency.
