Anxiety in Traditional Chinese Medicine

Anxiety refers to excessive worry, nervousness, or restlessness.

Common Symptoms

rapid heartbeat
insomnia
restlessness
difficulty concentrating
digestive discomfort.

Common TCM Patterns

Heart Blood Deficiency
Insufficient nourishment of the mind.

Liver Qi Stagnation
Emotional stress causing Qi blockage.

Heart and Kidney Yin Deficiency
Internal heat disturbing the mind.

Phlegm Heat Disturbing the Mind
Mental agitation and confusion.

Treatment focuses on calming the spirit, nourishing blood or Yin, and regulating Qi.
