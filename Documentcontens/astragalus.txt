Astragalus (Huang Qi)

Category
Qi tonifying herb.

Nature and Flavor
Slightly warm and sweet.

Meridians Entered
Lung and Spleen.

Functions
Tonifies Qi.
Strengthens immunity.
Raises Yang Qi.
Promotes tissue healing.

Indications
Chronic fatigue.
Weak immune system.
Frequent colds.
Slow wound healing.

Common Formulas
Bu Zhong Yi Qi Tang
Yu Ping Feng San.
