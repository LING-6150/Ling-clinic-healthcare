Digestive Disorders in Traditional Chinese Medicine

Digestive disorders include bloating, acid reflux, poor appetite, and irregular bowel movements.

Key Organs Involved

Spleen
Stomach
Liver

Common Patterns

Spleen Qi Deficiency
Weak digestion and low energy.

Food Stagnation
Overeating or poor diet leading to digestive blockage.

Liver Overacting on Spleen
Stress affecting digestion.

Damp Heat in Stomach
Burning sensation, bad breath, and acid reflux.

Treatment principles include strengthening the spleen, regulating stomach Qi, and clearing dampness.
