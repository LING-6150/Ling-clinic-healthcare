Dang Gui (Angelica Sinensis)

Category
Blood tonifying herb.

Nature and Flavor
Warm, sweet, and acrid.

Meridians Entered
Liver, Heart, Spleen.

Functions
Nourishes and tonifies blood.
Regulates menstruation.
Invigorates blood circulation.
Moistens the intestines.

Indications
Irregular menstruation.
Menstrual pain.
Blood deficiency.
Fatigue and dizziness.
Dry constipation.

Common Formulas
Si Wu Tang
Ba Zhen Tang
Dang Gui Bu Xue Tang.

Notes
Often used in women's health formulas and fertility treatments.
