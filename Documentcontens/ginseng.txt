Ginseng (Ren Shen)

Category
Qi tonifying herb.

Nature and Flavor
Warm and sweet.

Meridians Entered
Lung, Spleen, Heart.

Functions
Tonifies primordial Qi.
Strengthens spleen and lung.
Promotes fluid production.
Calms the spirit.

Indications
Fatigue.
Shortness of breath.
Weak digestion.
Chronic illness recovery.

Common Formulas
Si Jun Zi Tang
Shen Ling Bai Zhu San.

Notes
Considered one of the most powerful Qi tonics in Traditional Chinese Medicine.
