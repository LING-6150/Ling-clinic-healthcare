Infertility in Traditional Chinese Medicine

Infertility is defined as the inability to conceive after regular unprotected intercourse for one year. In TCM, reproductive health is closely linked to Kidney essence, Liver blood, and overall Qi balance.

Common Causes in TCM

Kidney Essence Deficiency
Low reproductive energy and hormonal imbalance.

Liver Qi Stagnation
Stress affecting menstrual cycle and ovulation.

Blood Stasis
Poor circulation affecting the uterus.

Spleen Deficiency
Poor nutrient absorption affecting reproductive health.

Symptoms may include

irregular menstrual cycles
low back pain
fatigue
cold hands and feet
emotional stress.

Treatment focuses on nourishing kidney essence, improving blood circulation, and regulating hormones.
