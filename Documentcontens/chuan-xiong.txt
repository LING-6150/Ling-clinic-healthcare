Chuan Xiong (Ligusticum Chuanxiong)

Category
Blood invigorating herb.

Nature and Flavor
Warm and acrid.

Meridians Entered
Liver, Gallbladder, Pericardium.

Functions
Invigorates blood circulation.
Promotes Qi movement.
Relieves pain.

Indications
Headache.
Menstrual pain.
Blood stagnation.
Poor circulation.

Common Formulas
Si Wu Tang
Xue Fu Zhu Yu Tang.
