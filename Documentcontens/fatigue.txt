Fatigue in Traditional Chinese Medicine

Fatigue is a common complaint and may result from deficiency of Qi, Blood, or organ dysfunction.

Common Symptoms

low energy
shortness of breath
weakness
poor concentration
lack of motivation

Possible TCM Patterns

Spleen Qi Deficiency
Symptoms include fatigue after eating, bloating, poor appetite, and loose stool.

Qi and Blood Deficiency
Symptoms include dizziness, pale complexion, insomnia, and weakness.

Kidney Yang Deficiency
Symptoms include cold intolerance, lower back weakness, and chronic fatigue.

Dampness Accumulation
Symptoms include heaviness in the body, brain fog, and digestive issues.

Treatment focuses on strengthening digestion, boosting Qi, and improving circulation.
